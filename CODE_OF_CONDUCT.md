# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

LabFlow is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Focus on constructive criticism and learning
- Acknowledge and respect cultural differences

### Be Collaborative

- Work together toward shared goals
- Be open to feedback and willing to learn
- Share knowledge and help others succeed
- Credit others for their contributions

### Be Professional

- Respect the time and effort of maintainers
- Follow project contribution guidelines
- Use appropriate channels for communication
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

### Harassment

- Sexual comments, jokes, or unwanted advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threatening language
- Personal attacks or doxing
- Persistent off-topic disruption

### Discrimination

- Exclusion based on protected characteristics
- Stereotyping or discriminatory language
- Unequal treatment or opportunities

### Abuse of Power

- Using moderator/maintainer position to silence dissent
- Selective enforcement of guidelines
- Retaliatory actions against those reporting issues

### Bad Faith Participation

- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Bad faith arguments not seeking genuine discussion
- Promoting harmful or illegal activities

---

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

### How to Report

1. **Email**: Direct message [@tico-Lin](https://github.com/tico-Lin)
2. **Include**:
   - Description of the incident
   - Involved individuals or usernames
   - Screenshots if applicable
   - Date and time
   - Context and impact

### Confidentiality

- Your report will be treated confidentially
- You won't be retaliated against for good-faith reports
- Investigation details are private unless you consent to share

### Response Time

- Initial response within 48 hours
- Investigation and action plan within 1 week
- Updates provided as appropriate

---

## Enforcement

### Possible Actions

1. **Warning**: Private conversation about behavior change
2. **Temporary Ban**: Limited community participation
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from project and community

### Escalation

Serious incidents may be:

- Reported to appropriate authorities
- Escalated to platform administrators
- Addressed through legal channels if necessary
